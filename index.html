Frequently Asked Questions

What does an Import-Export Sales Executive do?

As an Import-Export Sales Executive, I manage international trade operations by identifying potential markets, negotiating with overseas clients or suppliers, handling export/import documentation, ensuring compliance with trade regulations, and closing profitable sales deals across borders.

Which markets or regions do you specialize in?

I have experience working with regions including [e.g., Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and North America], depending on the product segment and client demand. My market knowledge includes trade regulations, buyer behavior, and logistics challenges specific to these regions.

How do you find and develop international clients?

I use a combination of methods including:

  • Trade fairs and exhibitions
  • B2B platforms (e.g., Alibaba, TradeIndia, Global Sources)
  • Cold outreach via email and LinkedIn
  • Existing network and referrals
  • Government or Chamber of Commerce databases

How do you ensure smooth logistics and shipping?

By coordinating closely with freight forwarders, customs brokers, and warehouse providers, I ensure timely and cost-effective movement of goods. I also plan for contingencies and track shipments to update clients regularly.
